Ingredients

  • Beef chuck
  • Rice noodle
  • Seasoning packet
  • Ox tail
  • Onion
  • Ginger

Steps (approximate)

  1. Clean the ox tail
  2. Put ox tail in large pot with water & bring to low boil
  3. Heat seasonings, onion, and ginger in pan until aroma is present
  4. Place seasoning packet in pot with boiling ox tail
  5. Cook on low heat for 4 hours
