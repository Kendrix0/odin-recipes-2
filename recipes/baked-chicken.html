Oven-baked Chicken!

Description

Ingredients

  • Chicken drumsticks
  • Onion
  • Better than Bouillon
  • Garlic powder, paprika, other seasonings
  • Butter

Steps (approximate)

  1. Mix drumsticks and seasonings together then place in ceramic pot
  2. Add BTB and hot water to pot
  3. Cut onion and butter then put in pot
  4. Bake in oven 350 F for 1.5 hours
  5. Put oven on broil till chicken is browned
